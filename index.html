Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generation and never fails to delight

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium sized apple peeled and sliced
Instructions
  1. preheated oven to 375deg
  2. In a bowl mix the sugar and cinnamon
  3. Toss the apple in the sugar-cinnamon mixture
